# email2ppt — Quick Start

**Time needed:** 10 minutes.
**You'll need:** your invite from Shawn, your phone, and your Gmail.

---

## Step 1 — Open the portal

Tap the **portal link** in your invite email.
Click **Sign in with Google**. Pick the Gmail you want monitored.

---

## Step 2 — Connect your Gmail

Click **Connect Gmail**.
Click **Allow** on the Google permission screen.
Wait for the green "Gmail connected" message.

> 📷 *Screenshot here: the green "Gmail connected" confirmation.*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

---

## Step 3 — Get your Telegram code

Still in the portal, click **Get my Telegram link**.
A short **code** will appear (six digits, or a short string of letters and numbers).
**Copy it.**

> 📷 *Screenshot here: the portal screen showing your code.*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

---

## Step 4 — Open Telegram and find the bot

Open **Telegram** on your phone.
Tap the **search icon** (🔍 top right).
Type the **bot name** from your invite.
Tap the bot to open the chat.

> 📷 *Screenshot here: Telegram search results showing the bot.*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

---

## Step 5 — Send your code to the bot

In the message box, type **exactly this** — replace the example with your real code:

```
/start 483920
```

Press send.

> 📷 *Screenshot here: your message in the bot chat, just before sending.*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

---

## Step 6 — Confirm you're linked

The bot replies in a few seconds. You want to see this:

> ✅ **Linked your email2ppt account. Future alerts will arrive here.**

If you see that, **you're done.** 🎉

> 📷 *Screenshot here: the bot's "Linked" reply.*

&nbsp;

## Step 7 — Pick your priority senders

Go back to the portal. Open **Priority Senders**.
Add one or two email addresses to start (for example: `boss@yourcompany.com`).
Save.

> 📷 *Screenshot here: the Priority Senders screen with one address added.*

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

---

## You're set up

The next time one of your priority senders emails you, the bot will send you a short summary in Telegram, plus a clean PDF.

To test: ask a priority sender to send you a quick "hello." Within five minutes, you should see an alert.

# If something goes wrong

| What you saw | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| **"This link expired"** | Go back to the portal. Click **Get my Telegram link** for a new code. Try Step 5 again. |
| **"Already used by someone else"** | Same fix — get a fresh code from the portal. |
| **"That link doesn't look valid"** | You probably mistyped. Use the **Copy** button in the portal instead of typing by hand. |
| **Bot says nothing** | Wait one minute. If still nothing, take a screenshot and message Shawn. |
| **Pressed the blue START button by accident** | No problem. Just type `/start <your code>` as a new message. |

# Useful Telegram commands (optional)

You don't need these to use email2ppt. But they're handy:

- `/help` — list of commands
- `/whoami` — confirms you're still linked
- `/push` — get the morning summary right now
- `/unlink` — turn it off (e.g. for vacation)
